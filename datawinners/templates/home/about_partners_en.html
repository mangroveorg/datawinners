An international partnership of private sector companies, academic institutions and non-governmental organizations are working collaboratively to create and manage DataWinners.com as an open-source project. Human Network International (HNI) and ThoughtWorks, Inc. lead the effort to make this mobile data collection service responsive to the needs of development organizations worldwide.

Our goal is to create a global community, working together to make mobile data collection better. Click here to access the code base. We encourage partners to contribute code and ideas so DataWinners will continue to innovate.

We want to thank our partners, and beta-testers featured here. They contribute significantly to our goal of offering an online, multi-channel, data collection platform to development organizations worldwide.

ThoughtWorks

Software development partner

A global IT consultancy that delivers custom software, software tools, consulting, and transformation services to Global 1000 companies. ThoughtWorks personnel in Pune, India wrote the majority of the code for both Mangrove and DataWinners.
www.thoughtworks.com

Mule Design

Design studio

Mule Design is a San Francisco-based design studio that created the intuitive, user-friendly DataWinners interface
www.muledesign.com

Modi Research Group

Software development partner

The Modi Research Groups leads Columbia University’s Earth Institute’s efforts that cut across energy, rural infrastructure and development. Staff of the Modi Research Group provided valuable code and advice to the Mangrove and DataWinners projects.
modi.mech.columbia.edu

Praekelt Foundation

Software development partner

Praekelt Foundation builds open source, scalable mobile technologies and solutions to improve the health and wellbeing of people living in poverty. The Mangrove project uses Vumi a scalable conversation engine for the delivery of SMS, Star Menu and chat messages to diverse audiences.
www.praekeltfoundation.org

Airtel Madagascar

Technical partner

Airtel Madagascar is the island’s largest provider of telecommunication services. Airtel/Madagascar provided telecommunications advice to the DataWinners project.
www.africa.airtel.com/madagascar/

UNICEF/Madagascar

Beta tester

The world's leading organization protecting the rights of children and young people. UNICEF in Madagascar is using DataWinners to collect nutrition data and information on local radio programming.
www.unicef.org

Catholic Relief Services/Madagascar

Beta tester

CRS is the international humanitarian agency of the Catholic community in the United States. CRS provides relief in emergency situations and helps people in the developing world break the cycle of poverty through community-based, sustainable development initiatives. CRS is using DataWinners to collect data on water and hygiene projects in remote areas in Northeastern Madagascar.
www.crs.org

PSI/Madagascar

Beta tester

PSI is a leading global health organization with programs targeting malaria, child survival, HIV and reproductive health. PSI is using DataWinners to collect data for a large adolescent reproductive health project and stock information for a wide range of public health products.
www.psi.org
