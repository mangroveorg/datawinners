Human Network International (HNI) helps organizations to embrace, reshape and maximize the use of information and communication technology in innovative, productive and sustainable ways.

We are a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization based in Washington, D.C. with offices in Madagascar. HNI has received financial and in-kind support from a variety of sources including foundations and institutions based around the world.
