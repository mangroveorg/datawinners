How to Collect Data
Collect your data using any combination of Web, Android App and SMS. You choose the channels most convenient for you and your Data Senders:
1. Web: Fill out the Questionnaire online.
2. Android App: Download Questionnaires directly onto your Android Smartphone and Tablet.
3. SMS: Use any simple mobile phone to send in data using a single SMS. Send your SMS to the telephone number +44 786 003 4166. For the free Basic Account, international SMS rates apply. You pay to send your messages to DataWinners. DataWinners will send automatic confirmation and error messages back in response to your SMS. You can submit up to 50 messages during your 1-year period.
