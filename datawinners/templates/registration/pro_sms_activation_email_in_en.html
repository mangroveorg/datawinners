How to Collect Data
Collect your data using any combination of Web, Android App and SMS. You choose the channels most convenient for you and your Data Senders:

  1. SMS: Use any simple mobile phone to send data using a single SMS.
  2. Web: Fill out the Questionnaire online.
  3. Android App: Download Questionnaires directly onto your Android Smartphone and Tablet.
