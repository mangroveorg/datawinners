How to Send Data
DataWinners allows you to send your data 3 different ways. Choose the channel most convenient for you.

  1. Web
    Use any computer connected to the Internet to send in data. Log in to your DataWinners account to fill out the Questionnaire online.
  2. Android Smartphone
    Use any Android Smartphone to send data. Download the Questionnaires directly onto your phone. Log in to your DataWinners account for further instructions.
  3. SMS
    Use any simple mobile phone to send data using a single SMS. Ask your project manager for the SMS Questionnaire.
